About this project:


Classics 517, Ancient Religions of the Mediterranean, covers diverse topics in three broad areas: Greek religion, Roman religion, and the Rise of Christianity. The assigned readings, assignments, and exams throughout the semester are text-based, sourced from texts like academic studies and historical poems. This project aims to develop an interactive blog-style website that will bridge the gap between these text-based sources and supplementary academic visual information, such as illustrations, maps, or statues, that is available online, in hopes of creating lasting support in this course for myself and future students. The course consists of pre-lecture readings and writing assignments to gain knowledge about the lecture topics. By providing a visual parallel for these writing assignments and including analyses of how they are related, I will gain a more concrete and richer understanding of course topics. By complimenting the pre-existing materials and assignments, this project will be relevant to the course while introducing an intensive learning experience.
